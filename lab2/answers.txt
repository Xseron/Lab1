#Loop List
1. What is a correct syntax for looping through the items of a list?
Answer:

  print(x)

2. Insert the missing part of the while loop below to loop through the items of a list.

Answer: 
mylist = ['apple', 'banana', 'cherry']
i = 0
while i < len(mylist):

  i = i + 1
